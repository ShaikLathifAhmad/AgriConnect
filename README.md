🌾 AgriConnect: AI-Driven Crop Waste Reduction 🤖

📖 Case Study Overview
AgriConnect is an innovative AI solution conceptualized and developed during the NextWave AI Buildathon. This project serves as a modern, technology-driven case study focused on one of the agricultural sector's most critical challenges: post-harvest loss.

By blending an accessible, user-friendly web interface with powerful Python-based AI capabilities, AgriConnect aims to empower farmers, optimize supply chains, and drastically reduce the wastage of fresh vegetables and fruits.

🎯 The Problem: The Post-Harvest Crisis

Globally, a staggering percentage of fresh produce is lost between harvest and retail. For farmers, this translates to severe economic losses and wasted labor.

Lack of Predictive Insights: Farmers often harvest without knowing exact market demand, leading to oversupply and spoilage.

Storage Inefficiencies: Without data on estimated shelf-life, perishable goods rot before reaching the market.

Information Asymmetry: Farmers lack direct, real-time connectivity to buyers, leading to reliance on middlemen and prolonged transit times.

💡 Our Solution: AgriConnect

AgriConnect bridges the gap between traditional farming and modern technology. Our prototype currently features a robust Front-End interface paired with simulated AI features built in Python, serving as a comprehensive proof-of-concept for a larger, scalable ecosystem.
